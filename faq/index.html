How do I reserve/book a room?

You can either email us at reservations@goldenpeakhotel.com or text/call us at (+63)930-969-3610. You can also visit us and book in-person, check out our Google Maps

Where can I ask about details of your function rooms?

Just email us at sales@goldenpeakhotel.comor text/call (+63)930-699-4288.

Are there any extra fees for an extra person?

There is an extra fee of P800 for each person, this includes an extra bed and free breakfast.

What are your amenities?

We have our restaurant and business center at the ground floor, good internet connection, transportation services and many more. Please check out our amenities page for the full list.

Where's the nearest pharmacy?

– Rose Pharmacy Ayala Center Cebu
– Mercury Drug Ayala Center Cebu
(350-400 metres, 8 min walk)

How long does it take to travel between the airport and Golden Peak?

It takes around 30 minutes (by car), depending on the traffic.

What are some attractions/popular venues near Golden Peak?

Golden Peak’s location is very ideal. It is near the bustling Cebu Business Park, Ayala Centre, Cebu Country Club and many other locations.

Golden Peak Hotel and Suites

An affordable business hotel conveniently located at the center of Cebu’s commercial and financial district and within walking distance to major commercial and shopping areas.
